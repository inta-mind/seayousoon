The Place

A quieter kind of coastline

Methana sits apart from the well-worn island routes — a volcanic peninsula of pine and stone, thermal springs and small harbours, an hour from Athens by sea. Sea You Soon & Spa was built into this landscape rather than onto it.

Each villa opens onto its own pool and a wall of glass facing the water. Inside, the palette stays close to the ground it was drawn from — lime plaster, raw wood, woven textiles — so the sea remains the only thing competing for your attention. Walk five minutes and you're at the shoreline; stay on the terrace and the shoreline comes to you.

This is a place for slow mornings, long lunches, and the particular hospitality that only a small, family-run property can offer — spa rituals, quiet service, and a genuine wish that you leave rested.

  • Uninterrupted sea views
  • Private pool at villas
  • In-villa spa treatments
  • Five minutes to the sea on foot
